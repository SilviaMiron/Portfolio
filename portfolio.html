About Me

A few months ago, I decided to take a big leap—switching from my role as an administrative assistant to following my passion for technology. Becoming a mom made me realize I wanted a career that would challenge me, keep me learning, and offer flexibility. So, I took the plunge and completed a 12-week software development bootcamp, where I learned to build dynamic, user-friendly applications. I'm a highly motivated and curious person who loves problem-solving and continuous learning. I also speak four languages—English, Italian, Romanian, and Albanian—which has given me a deep appreciation for different cultures and perspectives. When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me reading or working on my first book! Now, I’m excited to start my journey in tech, eager to grow as a developer, collaborate with others, and keep expanding my skills. I can’t wait to see where this path takes me!
